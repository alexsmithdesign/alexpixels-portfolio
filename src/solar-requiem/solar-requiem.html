Main Characters

WYNTER

Liminal Knight

AGE 23
WEAPON Dual Sword
HOMETOWN Ra - City of the Sun

Born as princess of the Ra royal family, Wynter’s parents were killed under mysterious circumstances when she was a child. A man known as the Count gained control over the empire, and forced a young Wynter to pray to the Solar Obelisk in order to increase its Light Energy output. This put a lot of strain on her and she begins to lose her Solar powers. She has gone rogue as an adult and actively opposes the Holy Ra Empire, and the abuse of Light Energy.

Other Characters

COUNT HORVEK

TYRANT

Genius scientist who broke the boundaries of Light Energy usage and created machines that were too powerful for their own good, but was banished to the Impasse. To get revenge he returned with an army of robots and monsters who he manipulated with his genius. He authoritatively rules over Ra now in place of an actual emperor. 

NUON

PALADIN

His true origins are unknown, but Nuon was able to gain the trust of Count Horvek, and serves as a tutor to young Wynter.

STORY & SETTING

Synopsis

TAKE A STAND AGAINST THE LIGHT

The people rejected Dark Energy as part of the natural order, but now Light runs amok. As the world faces it's final days, three heroes must deliver a message of hope to the future that may undo the destruction of the past.

Vocabulary & Important Terms

Dark & Light Energy
The fundamental forces that make up the Universe, although, Dark Energy is heavily stigmatized.
Veyne
Goddess once believed to have created Planet Eris and Humans.
Eris
Earth-like planet orbiting the sun, Ra
Veyne's Observatory
Ancient satellite that once served as a shrine. Accessing it is strictly forbidden by the Empire. It is considered the closest place one can get to the Sun safely.
Solar Obelisks
Mysterious machines responsible for channeling energy from the sun, and controlling Light Energy in the Solar System.
Duality Institute
Institution founded by Saruna to study and teach the science and magic of Light and Dark Energy.
Holy Conservatory
An old Imperial institution that strictly teaches Light Energy and condemns all forms of Dark Energy. 
Ra
The name of the Sun, the Empire, and the City of Light.
The Impasse
The barbarian lands far outside the control of the Empire. People normally do not return, except for the Count and his army.
Pearls
Crystallized fragments of pure energy. Pearls are both currency and power source in the world of Solar Requiem.

BATTLE SYSTEM

“Character Focus Time Battle”

Inspired by Final Fantasy’s Active Time Battle. Specifically games like FF4, Lightning Returns FFXIII, and FF7 Remake.

Alternate your focus between 3 playable characters. Strategize whose ATB to use and when.

Each ability drains the ATB gauge partially.

Attack in rapid succession to build chains and stagger enemies.
