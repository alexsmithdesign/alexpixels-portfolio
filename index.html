SOLAR
REQUIEM

A PIXEL ART JRPG ABOUT 3 HEROES MAKING A FINAL STAND AGAINST THE LIGHT

The people sealed dark energy away, but now Light runs amok. As the world faces it's final days, three heroes must deliver a message of hope to the future that may undo the destruction of the past.

Solar Requiem is a JRPG being developed in Godot Engine.

ERIU

SUN PRINCESS

AGE HEIGHT WEAPON
23 6'1" SWALLOW

Orphaned as a child, Eriu was raised in the Temple of Light by the Light Priestess. She has trained in the arts of Neutral Energy, while aiding the Temple in it's research efforts.

ATUM

DARK THEORIST

AGE HEIGHT WEAPON
56 7'2" SCYTHE

Atum and the Light Priestess work together to study Dark Energy and to better understand the realm which rejects the Dark. He tends to be reserved and intimidating, but cares deeply about his work and other people.

SARUNA

LIGHT PRIESTESS

AGE HEIGHT WEAPON
48 4'9" STAFF

Known for her benevolence, and big personality, Saruna controls one of the Largest Light Temples in the realm which houses orphans, and conducts research. She often clashes with other followers of the Light who she deems "fakes."

ABOUT

ALEX

DESIGNER

I grew up on NES, SNES, and PS1 — an era where artists were solving impossible problems in a handful of pixels. When the industry moved on to 3D and HD, and threw out the past, I continued to respect 2D. I kept drawing.

My obsession made me want to create my own worlds. I started studying the history and art behind the games and shows I loved.

I sell pixel art assets and am expanding into physical products. I dream constantly of building worlds and art that resonate, and I share my work with a wonderful community that loves these same passions.
