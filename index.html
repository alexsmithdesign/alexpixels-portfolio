ABOUT

ALEX

DESIGNER

Alex has been drawing since he was small child and hasn't stopped since then. Today, his creative skillset ranges from traditional graphic design, illustration, pixel art, development, and much more.

Alex has worked for companies like Sumo, Visa, Pushnami, creating design systems, applications, websites, branding, merch, and pretty much anything that requires a designer's touch.

Alex sells assets online, is expanding into physical products, and is working on multiple personal projects. He never stops creating, and he dreams constantly of building worlds and designs that delight and excite.
